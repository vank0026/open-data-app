Project Brief

Roger van Koughnett

Description: All Parklands

This app will provide a map of Ottawa, on which all the parklands will be diplayed at their correct longitude and latitude.

It will be possible to click on each location to get more information, such as the place name and street adress.

There will also be an Admin section not available to all users where new entries can be made, and old entries can be edited or deleted.

User Persona: The Ultimate Frisbee Player

Imagine a world in which there were no parks. Many could get by without any trouble, but the Ultimate Frisbee Player would have trouble. Runners could run in the steets, and clidren play in schoolyards, but the frisbeeist requires a park in which to play his or her chosen game. These ultimate players are typically young and of middle class income with plenty of time to spare. They may be students or young proffesionals, and either men or women.
