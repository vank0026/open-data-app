Roger van Koughnett

Description: Community Gardens Finder

This app will provide a map of Ottawa, on which all the Community Gardens will be diplayed at their correct longitude and latitude.

It will be possible to click on each location to get more information, such as the place name and street adress.

There will also be an Admin section not available to all users where new entries can be made, and old entries can be edited or deleted.

User Persona: The Hipster Vegan

Imagine a world in which there were no vegitable gardens. Many could get by without any trouble, but the Hipster Vegan would have trouble. The average person can buy organic produce at the local store or farmer's market. But a hipster is too cool for such things. Talullah, our hipster friend, distains the mainstream markets and middle class stores; She needs her carrots from a garden cultivated by her own hands. The most indie you can get is a communal garden you grow yourself.
